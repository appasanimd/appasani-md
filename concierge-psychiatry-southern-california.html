Southern California runs on visibility — and the people who live at its center often have the least room to be openly unwell. The studio executives and talent of Los Angeles, the families of Malibu and Beverly Hills, the founders of Orange County, and the physicians and entrepreneurs of San Diego all need the same thing: sophisticated psychiatric care that is completely private.

I bring the full spectrum of integrative psychiatry to Southern California through discreet visiting in-person care and secure virtual continuity. As a California-licensed physician, I can see established patients anywhere in the state — with the same depth and discretion that defines the Presidio practice.
