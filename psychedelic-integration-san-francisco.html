The experience is only half the work

A profound experience can open a door — but whether anything lasting comes through it depends on the preparation beforehand and the integration afterward. That is the work I do: helping you make meaning, and translate insight into real change in your life.

I bring a psychiatrist's rigor and a research lineage with MAPS to this work, offered in person at the Presidio in San Francisco and virtually for established patients in California.

What integration looks like

  • Preparation — intention, safety, and psychological readiness
  • Integration — structured sessions to process and embody what arose
  • Whole-person context — held within your broader psychiatric care, not in a vacuum

Note: integration therapy supports your process before and after experiences; it is provided within the bounds of the law and sound medical practice.
