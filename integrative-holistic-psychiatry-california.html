Beyond the fifteen-minute med check

Most psychiatry treats a symptom. Integrative psychiatry treats the whole person — combining the rigor of Western medicine with nutritional, metabolic, contemplative, and Eastern healing practices into a single, coherent plan.

I practice this way across California: in person at my Presidio sanctuary in San Francisco, by private home visit in the Bay Area, and through secure virtual care for established patients statewide.

Four dimensions, one person

  • Mind — psychotherapy and the narrative work of understanding yourself
  • Body — metabolism, nutrition, sleep, hormones, and inflammation
  • Brain — precise pharmacology and neuromodulation
  • Spirit — meaning, contemplative practice, and psychedelic integration

Who it's for

Questions

Good to know.

What does "integrative psychiatry" actually mean? +
Treating the whole person rather than a single symptom — combining evidence-based Western medicine and psychotherapy with nutritional, metabolic, contemplative, and Eastern healing practices in one plan.
Do you offer holistic psychiatry virtually in California? +
Yes. Secure virtual visits are available for established patients anywhere in California, with in-person care at the Presidio in San Francisco and private home visits in the Bay Area.
Is this still real, evidence-based medicine? +
Yes. I am a board-certified psychiatrist. Holistic and integrative methods are layered onto rigorous medical training, not used in place of it.
