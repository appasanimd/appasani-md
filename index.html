What is concierge integrative psychiatry? +
Concierge psychiatry is a membership-style model with a deliberately small patient panel. That means longer appointments, direct access to your physician between visits, and genuine continuity of care. "Integrative" means treating the whole person combining evidence-based Western medicine with nutritional, metabolic, contemplative, and Eastern healing practices, rather than treating a single symptom in isolation.
What conditions do you treat? +
Depression, anxiety, treatment-resistant mood disorders, trauma and PTSD, ADHD, burnout, addiction and substance use, and the everyday struggles of high-performing, thoughtful people who feel that something deeper is off. Care is tailored to you, not to a diagnostic label.
What is neuromodulation and TMS? +
Neuromodulation uses targeted, non-invasive technology to regulate the brain's electrical activity. Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) is an FDA-cleared, drug-free treatment that uses gentle magnetic pulses to reactivate the mood circuits involved in depression and related conditions — with no anesthesia and no downtime. It is especially valuable when medications haven't worked or aren't well tolerated. Learn more about neuromodulation →
Do you accept insurance? +
The practice is out-of-network, which preserves the time, privacy, and depth that concierge care requires. Many patients receive partial reimbursement through their plan's out-of-network benefits, and a detailed superbill is provided. Contact the office to discuss fees and membership.
Where is the office, and do you offer virtual visits? +
The practice is located in the Presidio of San Francisco. In-person concierge care is the heart of the work, with secure virtual visits available for established patients in California, Colorado, New York, Connecticut, and Florida.
What happens in a first consultation? +
Far more than a typical intake. The initial consultation is an extended, unhurried evaluation across all four dimensions: mind, body, brain, and spirit to understand your history, biology, and goals. You'll leave with a clear, integrated plan and a real sense of the partnership ahead.
