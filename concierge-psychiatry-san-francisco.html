San Francisco asks a great deal of the people who power it — founders, operators, investors, artists, and the families behind them. Conventional psychiatry answers with fifteen-minute appointments and a prescription. I built something different.

My practice sits in the Presidio: 1,500 acres of forest and coastline on the edge of the city, where the setting itself helps your nervous system settle before we speak. For those who would rather not travel, I also offer private home visits across the Bay Area — Pacific Heights, the Marina, Presidio Heights, Sea Cliff, and down the Peninsula to Hillsborough and Atherton.

Beyond the city, I see patients across Northern California — Marin, wine country, Silicon Valley, and the Monterey Peninsula — and throughout Southern California.
